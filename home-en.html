Why systemic consulting?

When things feel out of balance, when direction is unclear, or change feels daunting, it helps to take a step back and look at the whole system. Whether you’re leading a team, guiding a change process, or navigating a personal challenge, systemic consulting creates space to understand what’s really going on beneath the surface. Together, we uncover patterns, clarify priorities, and open new ways forward: resource-oriented and solution-focused.

The goal? Sustainable, positive change. Greater self-efficacy. And stronger problem-solving skills—whether you're an individual, a team, or a whole organization. We look at the big picture to help you shift dynamics, tap into potential, and create lasting impact.
Think of a system like a mobile hanging from the ceiling - when you touch one piece, the whole structure shifts.

About me

I’m a certified systemic consultant, working with individuals, teams, and organizations to navigate change and develop more effective ways of working together.

I’ve always been curious about people – how we communicate, how we work together, and how we navigate change. That curiosity is what led me to systemic consulting, and it continues to guide my work today.

My background is rooted in project and event management - over 20 years of supporting complex, international projects taught me how teams function under pressure, and how essential communication and trust are for collaboration.

Process development
for teams and organizations

Organizational change is rarely straightforward. Whether you're growing, restructuring, or facing communication challenges, change brings complexity - and it affects more than just workflows.

My path to consulting

Bridging strategy and practice

Years of managing events sharpened my ability to define goals, respond to the unexpected, and navigate the complexity of group collaboration. These same skills now shape my approach as a consultant: grounded, solution-oriented, and always connected to real-life dynamics.

An international point of view

Living and working internationally has deepened my appreciation for cultural diversity—not just in theory, but in everyday experience. I’ve seen how different perspectives can spark creativity and innovation, and I’ve also learned how much care, curiosity, and communication it takes to truly connect across cultures.

From interaction to insight

My experience working with diverse stakeholders - clients, teams, leadership - helps me tune into different needs and facilitate constructive dialogue, even when interests differ.
